About Me

I am a third-year Electronics and Communications Engineering student at Pandit Deendayal Energy University, Gandhinagar, with a strong interest in semiconductor devices and next-generation electronics.

My research focuses on wide-bandgap semiconductor technologies, particularly GaN-HEMT devices, which I model and simulate using Silvaco ATLAS. I am also developing an innovative footstep power generation system exploring energy harvesting at the micro-scale.

Beyond academics, I serve as the 14th President of Rang — the Fine Arts Club of PDEU — and previously as Vice-President of Alchemy, the Chemistry Club, which I represented at India's largest Chemical Engineering Symposium at IIT Bombay.
